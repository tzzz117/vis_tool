Reference Map

this visualization shows relationship between reference and title/attribute/keyword similarity on specific year. each circle represents a paper, each curve line represent a relationship from paper referenced to paper referencing it.
you can get the distribution among papers with similarity based on title/abstract/keyword feature (feature obtained from mapping high dimension to 2d) showing the first ?% similar papers
when % >15%, most of points in one graph begin to appear in another graph with same percentage. while reference itself seems to have no relationship with similarity in title/abstract/keyword. (e.g. a paper is similar to an outlier)
conclusion - it's not necessary to reference to a paper with high similarity in title/abstract/keyword
